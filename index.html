About Asheville

Asheville is one of our favorite places! It's a vibrant and eclectic mountain city nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains. It has a rich arts, culinary, and craft beer scene, plus offers a variety of outdoor activities from hiking to rafting. Often considered "hippie" and "quirky" in its vibe.

Update as of 10/14 - Many of you are probably aware that western NC, including Asheville and Hendersonville, has been majorly impacted by Hurricane Helene. The devastation of this community is truly saddening; homes flooded, small businesses destroyed, infrastructure/roadways disrupted, and some small rural towns competely wiped. Please consider donating toward recovery efforts for western NC and/or other communities impacted by Hurricanes Helene and Milton (some links below), even instead of wedding gifts. We hope that our wedding can play a small part toward their economy bouncing back since this area relies on tourism. Please be patient as you make your accommodations.
